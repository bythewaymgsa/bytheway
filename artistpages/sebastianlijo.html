Sebastian Lijo MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Sebastian Lijo

My thesis consists of a series of mixes as part of a recurring radio program, named SoundMind Radio. The last mix of the set is curated by classmates and close friends., accompanied by a book filled with their respective responses about their specific relationships to music. Each mix is also realized as physical ephemera, with full designed packaging.

Sebastian Lijo is a multi-disciplinary designer and artist based in New Jersey. He is passionate about exploring the intersections of culture, the natural world, and aesthetics. With a strong curiosity for discovering new means of working, each project is approached with a meticulous consideration for detail.
