Merna Ahmed MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Merna Ahmed

An exploration of rest as resistance to the idea that our bodies are only worth the amount of labor they produce. My project emphasizes the impact of capitalist standards in relation to how we rest through satire and a pseudo waiting room, a space where people are forced to do nothing.

Hi, my name is Merna! I am double concentrating in design and photography. In my practice, I enjoy exploring the themes of my identity, community, and progressive ideals in my practice. I'm always excited to find ways to integrate illustration, photo and design together!
