Olivia Scarrott MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Olivia Scarrott

My project is about learning to reincorporate play as a tool for learning in our everyday lives. It takes the form of a game that makes it fun to experiment with basic forms and colors. It's intention is to help us recognize the value of going back to the basics and taking another look at the components of the world around us.

Hi, my name is Olivia and I am a graphic designer living and working in the New York/New Jersey area. I am a multidisciplinary artist working in graphic design, photography and printmaking. I am interested in the topics of interaction, learning and play and incorporate one or many of thesis topics in my work.
