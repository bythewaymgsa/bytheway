Olivia Scarrott MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Olivia Scarrott

My project is about learning to reincorporate play as a tool for learning in our everyday lives. It takes the form of a game that makes it fun to experiment with basic forms and colors. It's intention is to help us recognize the value of returning to the basics and taking another look at the basic components of the world around us.
