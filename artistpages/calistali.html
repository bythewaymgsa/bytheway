Calista Li MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Calista Li

With graduation nearing, I’ve thought about all the memories that were made and how the people in my life have impacted me in their own special way. They’ve become a part of my growth, and they are something that I hold close to my heart. For my final thesis project, I wanted to say thank you to those who have played a big part in my life. I designed “Thank You” cards to those in my life that have impacted me, and express what I am thankful for from each of them individually. I often find myself thinking about where I would be without them, and how they’ve led me to where I am today.

I am a Senior at Rutgers University - New Brunswick majoring in Design at Mason Gross School of the Arts. I have a strong passion in finding new ways in art to communicate with my audience, and design has specifically allowed me to explore the several different ways I can communicate my own thoughts and ideas. I love designing work related to my interests, such as politics and the NBA.
