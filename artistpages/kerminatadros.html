Kermina Tadros MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Kermina Tadros

As an Egyptian immigrant, being bicultural is an incredibly crucial topic. “In the Middle” communicates how living between two vastly different cultures can be a heavy burden on one’s shoulders. It is also a reminder that as difficult as it can be, being bicultural results in beautiful & unique individuals.

Kermina Tadros is a Coptic Orthodox graphic/web designer & photographer. Her work ranges from exploration of identity & pop culture to discussing the Egyptian/Coptic Orthodox community & its culture & traditions. Kermina’s ultimate goal is self expression & allowing that to go in conversation with various topics.
