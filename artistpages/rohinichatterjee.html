Rohini Chatterjee MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Rohini Chatterjee

A multi-faceted exhibition experience that aims to explore and identify the different ways we show moral support in hopes of facilitating an atmosphere where we can build stronger relationships with the people around us.

Two of the things I've always felt most passionate about are people and stories. Understanding people and their experiences, feelings, and perspectives is what enriches our lives. We all have a story to tell, and I believe they can be told through creative endeavors and technological innovation. I hope to take what I've gathered from others along with a few stories of my own to craft an empathetic and inclusive future fueled by creativity.
