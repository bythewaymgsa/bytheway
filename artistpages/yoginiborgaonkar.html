Yogini Borgaonkar MGSA Design Thesis Show 2022

Yogini Borgaonkar

A series of interactive objects that translate physical sensation into digital visualizations.

The manual movements of “push”, “pull”, & “twist” are placed adjacent to their digitized counterparts to “resensitize” attention to forgotten, everyday interactions.

Yogini Borgaonkar is a designer interested in creating enjoyable physical and digital products for service through interaction and storytelling.
